How to Track Your Rocket League MMR and Session Stats

Bakkboard tracks your Rocket League performance automatically — W/L, win rate, streak, MMR delta and full rank history — filtered by playlist and time period, without any account or login.

MMR trends, W/L timeline and play-style analytics in Bakkboard.

Session stats that reset when you want

The Stats tab shows your current session performance: matches played, wins, losses, win rate, streak and MMR delta. You control when a session starts — there is a "Clear session" button to reset the counters whenever you begin a new grind, and the previous session data is preserved in history.

Stats can be filtered by period and by playlist (1v1, 2v2, 3v3 — or all playlists combined). Every filter combination updates the numbers and charts instantly.

MMR delta overlay on screen

If you use the Stats Bar overlay, your current MMR change for the active playlist is visible on screen at all times. It updates after every match and adapts automatically when you switch between playlists — no manual input needed.

The Rank Badge overlay shows your exact MMR value alongside the rank icon and division, so you always know precisely where you stand.

MMR progression charts

The Analytics tab shows a Tracker.gg MMR progression chart for your tracked account — the full rank history over any date range you choose. You can compare multiple playlists on the same chart or focus on a single one.

Below the progression chart, a W/L timeline shows every match result as colored bars so you can spot streaks, tilt sessions and peak-performance periods visually.

Play-style trends

Bakkboard also tracks play-style metrics over time: shot accuracy, save rate, demo rate, possession percentage, touch count per match and more. Each metric is shown as a rolling average across your recent matches so you can see how your style evolves as you climb.

Player tracking — monitor specific opponents

If you encounter a player frequently — whether an opponent or a teammate — you can add them to your Tracked Players list. Bakkboard will automatically display a notification badge when that player appears in your lobby, so you are always prepared before kickoff.

Player tracking panel with encounter badges and quick stat lookups.

All data stays local

Every match, stat and ranking snapshot is saved to %LocalAppData%\Bakkboard on your machine. No data is sent to any external server except optional Tracker.gg lookups that you trigger by opening a player profile. Nothing requires a Bakkboard account or login.

Multi-account support

If you play on more than one account, Bakkboard tracks each independently. Switch between accounts from the settings panel at any time. If an account was renamed, you can merge its histories to keep everything in one place.
