Rocket League Live Match Stats — Everything on the Field, in Real Time

Bakkboard gives you a full live scoreboard during every Rocket League match: detailed stats for every player, Tracker.gg rank lookups, a real-time event log, ball possession and win probability — all updating as the game plays out.

Live scoreboard with full stats and Tracker.gg ranks for every player.

Full scoreboard, not just the score

The Rocket League in-game scoreboard shows points and a few basic stats. Bakkboard's live scoreboard goes further: for every player in the match you see goals, assists, saves, shots, touches, crossbars, demos and total points — all updating in real time as events happen.

Both teams are shown side by side with their team colors. The score at the top updates on every goal, and the scoreboard persists even after players leave mid-match so you do not lose data.

Tracker.gg rank for every player in the lobby

Next to each player's name, Bakkboard shows their Tracker.gg rank and peak rank for the current playlist — the same rank data you would look up manually. It fetches this automatically when the match starts.

Platform badges (Epic, Steam) appear next to each player's name so you can tell at a glance where they are playing from. Players who leave mid-match stay in the scoreboard with their stats preserved.

Event log with goal speeds and hard hits

Below the scoreboard, a real-time event log records every meaningful event in the match: goals with speed, assists, saves, hard hits, crossbars and demos — each stamped with the in-game time. Kickoff dividers separate each period of play so the log stays readable across a full match.

When you review a past match from history, the event log is preserved exactly as it was during the live game.

Ball possession bar

A live possession bar shows the percentage of time each team has had the ball during the current match. It updates continuously as the game progresses and uses each team's color so it is readable at a glance.

Win probability

Alongside the scoreboard, Bakkboard calculates a live win probability that updates every second. It blends a calibrated statistical model based on current score, time remaining, momentum, shot conversion, possession, touches and demos with your own historical data from similar past matches. The result is a realistic read of your team's chances as the match evolves.

Match history — replay any past match

Every match is saved automatically. You can reopen any past game from the Match History tab and see the complete scoreboard, event log, possession data and rank snapshot exactly as they were during the live game. Matches can be excluded from analytics or win probability training without deleting them.
